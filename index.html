About me

I am a postdoctoral fellow in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at Auburn University under the guidance of Prof. Yanzhao Cao. Prior to this appointment, I was a short-term Postdoctoral Associate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Cornell University, advised by Prof. Vikram Krishnamurthy. I received my Ph.D. in Mathematics from the University of Connecticut under the supervision of Prof. George Yin. I obtained my B.S. in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2018.
