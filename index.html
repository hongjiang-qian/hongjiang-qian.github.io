About me

I am a postdoctoral fellow in Department of Mathematics and Statistics at Auburn University under the guidance of Prof. Yanzhao Cao. I completed my Ph.D. in mathematics at University of Connecticut under the supervision of Prof. George Yin. Before that, I received my B.S. in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics at Huazhong University of Science and Technology, in 2018.
